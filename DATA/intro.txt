The game:                                                            
 - Reach the green glyph at the end of the level                     
 - Shoot bonus boxes to gain more points/time/speed                  
                                                                      
Controls:                                                            
 - ZQSD or arrow keys => upward/backward/strafe left and right     
 - Mouse axis => orientation                                             
 - Shoot => left mouse button                                       
 - F1 => pause/unpause the game and unlock/lock the mouse             
 - F11 => toggle fullscreen mode                                      
                                                                      
                                      HAVE FUN, MERRY CHRITMAS !
